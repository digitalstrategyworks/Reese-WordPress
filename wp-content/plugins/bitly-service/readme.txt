Bit.ly Service allows you to generate a bit.ly or j.mp shortlink for post, pages and custom post types.

== Description ==

With *Bit.ly Service* you can use [Bit.ly](http://bit.ly/), or even better, [Bit.ly Pro](http://bit.ly/pro/) to generate shortlinks for all of your blogs Posts, Pages and Custom Post Type.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the bitly-service.zip folder.
1. Upload the bitly-service folder to your /wp-content/plugins directory.
1. In your WordPress dashboard, head over to the Plugins section.
1. Activate Bit.ly Service.
